FAQ

Fair questions.

How is this different from Apple’s Screen Time limits?

Screen Time limits show a “Remind me in 15 minutes” button — a door you can always open. ARCO blocks apps only during focus sessions you start on purpose, and in Strict mode there is no door: the session can’t be ended until the timer runs out.

Can I get around the block?

In a normal session you can end focus anytime — ending it is a decision, not a reflex. In Strict mode you can’t end early from the app, the lock screen, or the widget. Breaks still work; quitting doesn’t.

What happens to my data?

Nothing leaves your iPhone. There’s no account and no server. Screen Time data is read through Apple’s framework, which physically prevents apps from exporting it.

Which apps can I block?

Any app installed on your iPhone, and whole categories. You pick them once — the same list powers your insights and your blocking.

Do I need Pro?

The planner, focus timer, streaks, and this-week insights are free forever. Pro is for the teeth: actual app blocking, Strict mode, unlimited habits, and deep history.
