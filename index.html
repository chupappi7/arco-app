QUESTIONS

Fair questions.

How is this different from Apple's Screen Time limits?

Screen Time asks nicely, and you tap "remind me in 15 minutes" a thousand times. ARCO connects a plan for the hour with a lock on the apps that steal it. During a focus session your chosen apps simply do not open, and strict mode removes the exit entirely.

Does the alarm really ring on silent?

Yes. ARCO uses Apple's alarm framework, the same system power the Clock app has. Schedule Wake up on your timeline and a real full-screen alarm rings at that time, silent switch or not. Requires iOS 26.

What can the free version do?

Almost everything: the full planner, the wake up alarm, habits with streaks, focus sessions, and three fully blocked sessions every week. Pro removes the limits: blocking on every session, strict mode, unlimited habits, and your full history.

Can you see my screen time data?

No. There is no account and no server. Usage data is read on your device through Apple's Screen Time framework, which is built so the numbers never leave your iPhone. We couldn't see them if we wanted to.

What if I need a blocked app urgently?

Normal focus sessions can be ended from the app or lock screen at any time. Strict mode is the one you choose when you don't want that door to exist. You always pick which mode you start.
